Caring for your wig is simple:

🧴 Wash: Use a gentle wig shampoo in cool water every 8-10 wears
💆 Condition: Apply wig conditioner, leave for 2-3 minutes, rinse gently
💨 Dry: Gently blot with a towel, then air dry on a wig stand
🔥 Style: Use heat tools on low-medium setting (max 180°C)
🛏️ Storage: Store on a wig stand or in the box when not in use
